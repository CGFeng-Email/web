WHO WE ARE
Canway Co.,LTD was established in 2005,is a professional manufacturer and trading company specializing in design,product develop,sales and manufacturing of hairdressing tools.We are located in Guangzhou China,with convenient transportation access. The company has cooperated with many clients both from domestic and overseas, and has obtained consistently high praise from our customers since it was set up.
