Air Freight
provides fast and reliable air freight services nationwide.

Leading Air Freight Forwarder

As one of the leading air freight forwarders in the industry, we provide comprehensive services to commercial, industrial, retail, and private clients. As a cargo agencies of airlines, we not only offer competitive rates but also ensure sufficient cargo space for bulk shipments exceeding 100CBM.

Air Charter

Our expert team provides comprehensive support for charter flight services, accommodating various requirements for cargo capacity, routes, and specific needs. We are committed to delivering customized charter solutions tailored to meet our clients' unique logistics demands. This service is particularly well-suited for pre-scheduled shipments and oversized or specialized cargo.

Expedited Air Freight Service

For time-critical shipments, our Expedited Air Freight service provides the fastest and most reliable solution. We offer same-day pickup and prioritize the nearest available flight to ensure your cargo reaches its destination with maximum efficiency. Whether handling small parcels or large shipments, our experienced team tailors optimal flight plans to meet your specific requirements. With priority access to cargo space, direct routing to minimize transfers, and expedited handling processes, we ensure your goods are delivered on time. This service is ideal for high-value and time-sensitive shipments, offering businesses a dependable and efficient logistics solution.

Premium Air Freight Service

When you require the most cost-effective solution for shipping your goods, we can arrange professional consolidation services at our facilities tailored to your needs. Our air freight consolidation service enhances efficiency, streamlines processes, and optimizes costs. Designed with reliability and speed in mind, our secure and competitive solutions deliver shipments to global economic hubs or destinations worldwide within just three to five days. This service is particularly suitable for time-sensitive cargo.

Dangerous Goods Handling Service

Shenzhen Best Legend is a professional service provider specializing in transportation solutions for Dangerous Goods (DG cargo). Whether it’s self-balancing scooters, electronic products, powders, or liquids, our expert team can provide tailored advice and customized solutions. We also conduct comprehensive evaluations and comparisons of insurance, transportation networks, and safety standards. Additionally, we maintain close communication and collaboration with logistics companies to ensure that goods are delivered safely and on time to their destination.

Sea-air multimodal transportation

Modern multimodal transportation combines the cost advantages of sea freight with the time efficiency of air freight, offering a transport solution that is both economical and time-effective. It ensures your cargo arrives on time while significantly reducing transportation costs.
