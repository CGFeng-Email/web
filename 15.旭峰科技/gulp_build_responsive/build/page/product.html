A New Era of Green Office
The Future Face of Smart Meetings
Electronic conference table cards are intelligent devices that replace traditional paper table cards, typically consisting of a small electronic screen and a backend control system. Its biggest feature is the ability to remotely update display content, such as participant names, positions, conference topics, and other information. The traditional method requires printing, cutting, and installing a large number of paper table cards, while electronic table cards only require batch modification of information on the computer, and all table cards can be updated synchronously, saving time, effort, and environmental protection.
