Thrive Stories
01
Comprehensive Consultation
Through in-depth discussions with our consultation team, we take note of the specific requirements you have for the project. Factors like project size, functionality, and cost are accounted for by our experts to ensure you get the solutions you need.
02
Detailed Development
Based on the information gathered during the consultation, our design team starts work on creating the right ESL solution for the project. We consider all aspects during the design phase, making sure your demands are well-met.
03
Foolproof Sampling
Once we have your preferred design, we proceed to create functional samples within seven days for you to see how the final labels would appear and perform. We also make adjustments to the ESLs based on the feedback you provide regarding the sample.
04
Efficient Mass Manufacturing
After confirming the sample, our team moves on to making the molds and PCBA boards for mass production. With our production expertise and modern facilities, the entire production process takes about 15 days from start to finish.
05
Meticulous Quality Control
Seasoned quality inspectors scrutinize the products during the manufacturing process, paying close attention even to the smallest of details. Completed products also go through a series of tests to guarantee they perform on par with our elevated standards.
06
Fast and Secure Delivery
Once the completed products pass the final inspections, they are properly packed to prevent damage during shipment. We partner with well-vetted logistics companies to ensure the fast and safe delivery of your products.
