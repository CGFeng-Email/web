About Us

Grand Electronics Limited was established in 2019. As an independent supplier of electronic components, we are committed to providing customers with high-quality ICs, Capacitors, Resistors, Diodes, Transistors, MOS tubes, Connectors,Dc-dc,Ldo,Eeprom,FLASH,MCU semiconductors, and other electronic components.Our products are widely used in communication, network, digital television, household appliance, consumer electronics, automotive electronics, industrial equipment and instrument, etc.
We are experts in sourcing shortage, obsolete, hard-to-find electronic components, to solve the problems of component samples and small batch procurement for purchasers. All components are purchased from the original factory and authorized agents, to guarantee 100% new and original quality.
