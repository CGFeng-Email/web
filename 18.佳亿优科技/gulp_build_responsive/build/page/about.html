JOYYIYOU

JOYYIYOU TECH CO.,LTD. was located in Shenzhen China, established in 2020, We are a locksmith wholesale company that supply Car keys, Shells, Transponder chips, Smart keys, Fobiks, Keyless entry remotes, Chips, Flip key blades, Insert key blades, Silicone key cover, Keychain, Locksmith tools, Key programmer and other automotive accessories.
Our company has more than 10 employees working in dedicated offices and warehouses. We were cooperated with different factories and shipping agents under sea/air/land transportation. That let us can accept all OEM or aftermarket orders more Professional and convenient. We have the Professional equipment, technicians, sales staff and packaging staff, committed to each cooperation and each customers.
Currently, JOYYIYOU has exported to Domestic Market as Western Europe, North America, South Asia ,middle East and so on. We always focused on quality and customer service to improve customer satisfaction. We will initiate the second phase of our development strategy. Regards “Reliable quality，reasonable price,efficient production time and good after-sales service” as our tenet. We hope to cooperate with more customer for mutual development and benefits.
We look forward to all potential customers, don't hesitate to contact us!!
