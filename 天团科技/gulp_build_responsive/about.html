About us

Shenzhen Flyzon Technology Limited was founded in 2016, located in Shenzhen,China.

Our factory are professional on electronic products like AC charger/Adapter,Charging cable,Earphones,power bank,Smart fans ... manufacture.

Most of our items have passed UL,ETL,PSE,CE,FCC,ROHS,SAA,KC,UKCA...international certification.Battery have passed MSDS,UN38.3...Transport certificates.

Our factory have passed BSCI,ISO9001.

We enjoyed good prestige as quality stable, in-time delivery and good customer service.We are dedicated to providing OEM/ODM Service for each individual client, helping them succeed in their business.

We have a very strict QC team that ensure per piece of item 100% inspected and in good condition before delivery.

So far we have owned 500+ main customers that cooperated more than 5 years already.

Keep our customers brands/reputation in High praise and sustained development is our responsibility.

To be a Reliable /Valuable partner is our mission.
