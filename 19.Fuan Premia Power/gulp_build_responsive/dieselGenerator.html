Field Performance Of Diesel Generator

Environment Friendly
We pay special attention to environmental function when we design our gensets. We adopt advanced noise reduction measures and equipment, lower 15-35db(A) of genset noise level.
Reliable
Powered by CUMMINS, PERKINS, BAUDOUIN, YTO engines, coupled with STAMFORD, LEROY SOMER, or our own alternator, enables the generator set to start quickly, easy maintenance and operation.
Easy To Operation
Daily maintenance work can be performed on the two sides of the machine, and wide door allows you easily get in the machine. With lock fixed on control panel, the door can be locked during genset unused or running.
Easy To Transport
Compact size, easy to lift and saving space. With lifting hole on top of canopy, can be lifted easily during overhaul. With forklift operation hole on genset bottom, easy to transit, can be moved conveniently.
