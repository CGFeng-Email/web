About Us

FUAN PREMIA POWER CO.,LTD
FUAN PREMIA POWER CO.,LTD is one of the professional generator manufacturer in china, established in 2008 year,based factory covers an area of over 5,000 square meters. we dedicated ourselves to the production,R&D and maintenance of various Diesel generator set,.PMI POWER supplies generating sets ranging 5KVA—4,000KVA open type, soundproof type, trailer type, containerized type diesel generator sets, ATS Panel , Synchronizing Panel , Lighting Tower, Load Bank, Also we supply Alternators.
