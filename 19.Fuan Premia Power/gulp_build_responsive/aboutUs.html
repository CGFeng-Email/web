About Us

Generator SET MANUFACTURER

FUAN PREMIA POWER CO.,LTD is one of the professional generator manufacturer in china, established in 2006 year,based factory covers an area of over 5000 square meters. we dedicated ourselves to the production,R&D and maintenance of various Diesel generator set.
PMI POWER is firmly committed to the brand's philosophy of *Anytime Anywhere,Power Solutions". We always base our product design and innovative ideas on producing highly reliable and high-quality products. PMI POWER also has a strong professional technical research and development team with advanced production and assembly lines. We continue to offer a wide range of power equipments including low voltage and high voltage generators, We aim to provide customized power, professional and efficient energy solutions for different projects and applications to meet the diverse demands of global customers.

WE FOCUS ON THE TECHNOLOGY INNOVATION
AND ATTESTATION WORK

In recent years, to be compliant with the requirements of the environment protection, we have concentrated upon the technology innovation and attestation work. We are ISO 9001 and ISO 14001 CE,CCS,CCC certificated.
