Vic Market Research Co., Ltd., with thirteen years of experience, provides high-quality market research products and customized research to support your business strategy and decision-making process. We provide professional market research reports, market research reports, feasibility studies, IPO consulting, business plans, and other services to enterprises in the research fields of chemical materials, electronic semiconductors, automotive and transportation, equipment and consumables, mechanical equipment, consumer goods, agriculture, energy and power, construction, food and beverage, network and communication, software, and business services. Especially in the fields of chemical and mechanical engineering, we have built solutions for customers to obtain segmented product data that cannot be collected by official units such as statistical bureaus, customs, and associations. Our headquarters are located in China, with offices in Canada and the United States, providing real-time services worldwide.
