We are always committed to becoming the most trusted partner for our customers

Release time：2025-03-28 Category：Exhibition Activities

TAIRUI ELECTRONICS GROUP was established in 2017 as a processing and service-oriented enterprise engaged in the research and development, production, and sales of various emerging high-tech consumer electronics products and robots. It has established a product supply system with Shenzhen as its headquarters, integrating research, production, and sales. It has two research and development centers, one marketing center, and five factories (two domestically and three overseas). Its products are exported to more than 20 countries and regions around the world, covering more than 400 cities worldwide. It is one of the excellent enterprises in Shenzhen's "Made in China" strategy for going global. The company takes "professionalism, focus, value, and satisfaction" as its primary service goal, and is deeply engaged in the research and production of the Internet of Things, ecological chain cooperation, and humanoid robots. It has a complete product service system and core software and hardware technologies, and has won multiple patents and media honors.
