Weichuang Asia Limited.

Our company has multiple internationally recognized professional certificates such as FSC and SEDEX.

Our products are widely used in various industries such as food, cosmetics, electronics, etc. We have an experienced professional team dedicated to providing customers with the highest quality products and services.

Our production facilities are equipped with advanced machines and technology to ensure that our products meet the highest quality and durability standards. We offer a variety of paper boxes in different sizes, shapes, and designs to meet the diverse needs of our customers.
Our products are made of environmentally friendly materials and are recyclable, making them an ideal choice for consumers with strong environmental awareness.
We believe that our products can help your business grow and succeed. Our competitive prices, reliable quality, and excellent customer service have earned us a good reputation in the market. We are confident that we can meet your requirements and exceed your expectations.

Company Culture

Our Design & Prototyping capabilities in packaging and visual merchandising integrate cutting-edge technology and innovative design principles. From concept development through to final prototyping and usability testing, we ensure that each solution meets the highest standards of luxury and functionality.

Mission
Weichuang is the leader global service company operating in several different industries, managing a wide range of packaging, visual merchandising and retail solutions. Weichuang acts as an end-to-end integrated player being an essential part of the high-end brands’ value chain, by combining sustainable R&D, innovative design, and a global network of manufacturing and logistic partners.
Vision
Weichuang aims to be the leader, premium global partner of sustainable and innovative packaging, visual merchandising and retail solutions, empowering high-end brands to deliver exceptional customer experiences through cutting-edge solutions and unwavering commitment to sustainability.
Values
We believe in placing the customer at the centre of every action, innovatively and proactively anticipating their needs and desires. All our activities must support and enhance the unique ideneity of our clients.
