We are always committed to becoming the most trusted partner for our customers

Time：2025-03-19 Source：Lumosora established

Lumosora established in 2019, is a high-tech enterprise specializing in the R&D, production, and sales of silicone waterproof LED strips. Since its inception, the company has adhered to a talent-oriented philosophy and integrity-based business principles. By integrating advanced production technologies, management methodologies, and top-tier professionals, Navigation Lighting provides comprehensive solutions to clients, assisting them in overcoming technical challenges and accomplishing high-complexity product development and manufacturing.

Equipped with state-of-the-art production facilities and a highly skilled workforce, the company maintains its competitive edge in the fierce market competition, enabling rapid and sustainable growth. For Navigation Lighting, delivering premium products, superior services, exceptional quality, and unmatched credibility remains an enduring mission.

In recent years, the company has successfully partnered with numerous leading enterprises, providing tailored support. We are committed to becoming your most trusted long-term partner, dedicated to fostering mutual success through innovation and reliability.
