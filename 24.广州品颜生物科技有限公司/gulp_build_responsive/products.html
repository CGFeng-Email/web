Agrimer

Agrimer is located in Plouquerneau on the northern coast of Brittany, France. Since its establishment in 1994, it has been a lead over3er in the processing and development of Breton seaweed, engaged in the conversion of marine resources. In the field of cosmetic raw materials, develop extracts from seawater, seaweed, and halophilic plants. In addition, it also offers 100% ocean formula: using seaweed ingredients and ocean therapy to give cosmetic formulas innovative textures.
