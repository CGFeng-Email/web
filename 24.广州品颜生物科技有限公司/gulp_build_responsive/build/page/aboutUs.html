About PinYan
Guangzhou Pinyan Biotechnology Co., Ltd. is an agent for importing and selling personal care product raw materials. We specialize in representing high-quality personal care product raw materials worldwide and provide information, concepts, new raw materials, and technical services to customers in the daily chemical industry.
We are committed to providing green and natural raw materials, comprehensive technical support, and innovative product solutions. We use reliable agency channels to select high-quality raw materials from around the world for you, ensuring the quality and stability of our products.
Integrity in service, excellent quality, and reasonable prices are our unchanging business principles.
Through continuous screening and integration, we mainly provide products from 5 suppliers, namely Sinerga from Italy, Agrimer from France, Ingersoll Biotech, Keyichuang from Guizhou, and Dingchun from Guangdong.
