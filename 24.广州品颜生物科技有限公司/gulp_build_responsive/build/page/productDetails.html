Agrimer

Agrimer is located in Plouquerneau on the northern coast of Brittany, France. Since its establishment in 1994, it has been a leader in the processing and development of Breton seaweed, engaged in the conversion of marine resources. In the field of cosmetic raw materials, develop extracts from seawater, seaweed, and halophilic plants. In addition, it also offers 100% ocean formula: using seaweed ingredients and ocean therapy to give cosmetic formulas innovative textures.

Erythcalm SC
Erythcalm SC is a natural active ingredient that can quickly relieve skin redness, repair skin barriers, and brighten skin tone. Its active ingredients (mainly lignans and terpenes) are extracted from Trifolium repens, which can effectively inhibit the activation of ADRB2, HRH1, TRPV1, and EDNRA, thereby reducing the release of vasodilator CGRP and alleviating skin redness caused by stimulation.
