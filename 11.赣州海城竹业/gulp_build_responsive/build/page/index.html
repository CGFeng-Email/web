about us

Ganzhou Haicheng Bamboo Industry Co., Ltd. was established in August 2020 and is located in Tianxin Industrial Base, Quannan County Industrial Park, Ganzhou City, Jiangxi Province. Relying on the abundant bamboo resources in southern Jiangxi, we focus on the research and production of bamboo sticks and bamboo flower sticks. Our products are exported to more than 30 countries around the world, with an annual production capacity of over 5000 tons. As a benchmark enterprise in the industry, we have obtained FSC certification and ISO 9001 system certification, practicing the concept of green manufacturing and providing environmentally friendly and traceable bamboo product solutions to global customers.
