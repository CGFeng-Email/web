We are always committed to becoming the most trusted partner for our customers

Release time：2025-04-08 Category：Exhibition Activities

Ganzhou Haicheng Bamboo Industry Co., Ltd. was established in August 2020 and is located in Tianxin Industrial Base, Quannan County Industrial Park, Ganzhou City, Jiangxi Province. The legal representative of the company is Mr. Lin Junhua, with a registered capital of 6 million yuan and an area of approximately 26 acres. The company utilizes the abundant bamboo resources in surrounding counties (cities) to mainly produce bamboo sticks and bamboo flower sticks, and mainly exports them abroad.

Bamboo sticks, bamboo flower sticks and other bamboo products are popular low-carbon and environmentally friendly necessities in the market. With the modernization of China's social economy, changes in people's lifestyles and the improvement of living standards, social demand will further increase. But currently, although there are many bamboo product enterprises, most of them are transitioning from family workshops to corporate operations, and there is an urgent need for influential benchmark enterprises to emerge to occupy the market high ground and play a leading role in the industry. At present, our company has the following advantages:

1. Specializing in making bamboo sticks and bamboo flower sticks, with rigorous enterprise management, traceable products, stable quality, and good industry reputation.

2. Having certain industry competitiveness and a stable team of professional talents and cadres.
3. Familiar with the market, strong development ability, and the ability to provide customized services for customers.
4. Excellent team, harmonious cooperation.
Our company combines advantages such as platform, technology, information, marketing power, capital, and talent. Our self built factory has become a large-scale enterprise, which is not only the demand of the current market and the development of the industry, but also an inherent requirement for the development of the bamboo industry. It is our common ideal and responsibility, aiming to make due contributions to the social and economic development.
